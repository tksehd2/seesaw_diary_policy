ガクスケ · Gakusuke

Turn school handouts into a family schedule.

ガクスケ helps parents and students photograph school prints, extract schedule information with AI, and register the results in Google Calendar. When the AI returns a map or other reference image, the app can also save that image to the user's Google Drive.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

What the app does

  • Photographs printed school notices, letters, and schedules with the device camera.
  • Uses AI to identify dates, times, events, reminders, and other schedule details.
  • Creates Google Calendar events so families can manage school schedules more easily.
  • Saves AI-returned map or guide images to Google Drive only when that output exists and the feature is used.

Google data access used

  • Google Calendar: create app-created calendar events, read calendar list information, and manage related calendar access settings needed for event registration.
  • Google Drive (drive.file): create and manage only the files created or opened by ガクスケ for saved AI output images.
  • Generative Language / Gemini scopes: use the user's available Google AI quota and related read-only tuning access required by the app's AI analysis workflow.

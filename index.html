About Seesaw Diary

Seesaw Diary is a specialized diary application that provides deep insights and warm counseling comments based on your daily records using advanced Google Gemini AI models.

  • Per-User Quota: We utilize your own Google account's free/personal quota for a cost-effective AI experience.
  • Local-First: Your diary entries are stored securely on your local device using SQLite.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Seesaw Diary ("the App") handles your data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App does not store or transmit your diary content to any external servers or third-party databases. All diary entries are stored exclusively within your device's local storage (SQLite).

2. Google API Data Usage

To provide AI features, the App requests your authorization to access the following Google API Scopes:

  • .../auth/generative-language.peruserquota: To access the Gemini model using your own personal quota.
  • .../auth/generative-language.tuning.readonly: Required for 2026-standard model invocation and personalized response generation.

Data accessed through these scopes is used strictly for generating diary comments and is never shared, sold, or used for any other purposes.

3. Data Security

Authentication is handled via official Google OAuth protocols. Access tokens are managed securely in-memory and are never permanently stored on external servers.

Terms of Service

1. Service Description

AI features in this App consume the free or paid quota assigned to your personal Google account. Any service interruptions due to quota exhaustion are governed by Google's policies and are not the responsibility of the App.

2. Limitation of Liability

AI-generated content is for informational and supportive purposes only and does not replace professional legal or medical advice. Users are responsible for backing up their own local diary data.

3. Permissions

Users can revoke the App's access to their Google account at any time via their Google Account Security settings.

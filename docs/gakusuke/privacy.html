ガクスケ / Gakusuke

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

ガクスケ is an app that converts photographed school prints into structured schedule information, then registers that information in Google Calendar. If the AI returns a map, handout image, or similar reference image, the app may save that image to the user's Google Drive. This Privacy Policy explicitly explains how our application accesses, uses, stores, and shares Google user data.

1. Data Accessed

The app requests only the Google API permissions necessary for its core functions. Specifically, we access:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.app.created: to create and manage calendar events created by ガクスケ.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.calendarlist.readonly: to read available calendar list information so the user can choose where events should be added.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.acls: to check or manage calendar access settings in connection with the calendar the app works with.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file: to create, open, or manage only the Drive files created or selected through the app, such as saved AI-returned map images.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/generative-language.peruserquota and https://www.googleapis.com/auth/generative-language.tuning.readonly: to perform AI analysis workflow and read the user's Google AI quota.

2. Data Usage

We use the accessed Google user data strictly to provide the app's functionality:

  • Captured school print images are analyzed to identify schedules, events, dates, times, and related school information.
  • Extracted schedule information is used to directly create or update entries in your Google Calendar.
  • If an AI response includes a map or reference image, that image may be saved to your Google Drive to support later viewing and sharing.
  • Google API access is used only for these in-app functions and never for advertising, tracking, or unrelated profiling.

3. Data Storage

How we store and protect your data:

  • No external server storage: ガクスケ does not store your Google Calendar data, Google Drive files, or personal information on our own external databases or servers.
  • Direct to Google: Calendar events and Drive files created by ガクスケ are stored directly and securely within your own Google account (Google Calendar and Google Drive).
  • Local Tokens: Authentication tokens used to access Google APIs are stored securely and locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • You retain full control over your data. You can delete the created events or files directly from your Google Account at any time.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Google user data obtained through these permissions is never sold, rented, or traded. It is not shared with unrelated third parties for advertising or any other purposes. Data is used solely to operate the requested features of ガクスケ.

Limited Use Policy Compliance:
ガクスケ's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will strictly adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Security and Revocation

The app is designed to limit Google data access to the minimum scopes needed. Access tokens and Google API permissions are used only for authenticated requests. The user may revoke Google account access for ガクスケ at any time by visiting their Google Account Security Settings.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the app's features or Google API usage change. Updated versions will be posted directly on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: tksehd2@gmail.com

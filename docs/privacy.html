Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Seesaw is strongly committed to your privacy. We strictly adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy. This document explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Accessed

To provide AI analysis features, Seesaw requests access to your Google account's Gemini API quota via the following specific scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/generative-language.peruserquota: To utilize your personal API quota.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/generative-language.tuning.readonly: For model interaction and response generation.

2. Data Usage

The accessed Google user data (API quota and interaction tokens) is used exclusively to provide the core AI analysis features within the Seesaw app. We do not use your data for profiling, advertising, or any other unrelated purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your Google user data with any third parties. Authentication tokens are managed securely in-memory and are never transmitted to external servers or shared with anyone else.

4. Data Storage & Protection

Seesaw is a entirely "Local-First" application. We do not store or transmit your diary entries, personal information, or API tokens to any external servers. All of your data remains securely encrypted and stored solely on your local device's database (SQLite).

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Because we do not store your data on our servers, you have full and absolute control over your data retention. Your data is retained only as long as you keep the app installed. To delete all your data permanently, simply uninstall the Seesaw application from your device. You can also revoke our application's access permissions at any time through your Google Account Security settings.

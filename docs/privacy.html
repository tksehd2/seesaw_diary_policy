Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

Seesaw is a "Local-First" application. We do not store or transmit your diary entries to any external servers. All data remains on your local device (SQLite).

2. Google API Usage & Scopes

To provide AI analysis, Seesaw requests access to your Google account's Gemini API quota via the following scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/generative-language.peruserquota: To utilize your personal API quota.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/generative-language.tuning.readonly: For 2026-standard model interaction and response generation.

Tokens are managed securely in-memory and are never shared with third parties.

3. Your Rights

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your Google Account security settings.

Welcome to Seesaw

Seesaw is a "Local-First" minimalist journaling application designed to give you a peaceful space for your thoughts. Your data is stored locally and backed up securely to your own personal cloud.

Why choose Seesaw?

  • Local-First Storage: Your active diary entries are saved directly to your local device (SQLite). We never transmit your private thoughts to our own external servers.
  • Google Drive Sync: Your data is securely backed up and synced directly to your personal Google Drive, giving you complete ownership of your backups.
  • Private AI Analysis: Seesaw securely requests access to your personal Google Gemini API quota to provide smart journal analysis without compromising your data privacy.
  • Complete Control: Because data is stored locally and backed up to your own Drive, you maintain 100% control of your data and permissions at all times.

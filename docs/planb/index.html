Plan B

Your personal archive for backup choices.

Plan B is an app for saving the next best option when your usual choice is unavailable. You can store photos, images, and text together so your backup options are easy to revisit later.

Saved content can be backed up to and restored from Google Drive, and the app also includes a polished screenshot feature for sharing.
